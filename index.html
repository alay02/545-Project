About Hoboken

Hoboken, New Jersey, located on the west bank of the Hudson River across from Manhattan, is a city with a rich history and vibrant culture. Known for its historic waterfront, which was once a major shipping hub, Hoboken offers stunning views of the Manhattan skyline. The city is often recognized as the birthplace of baseball. With parks like Pier A Park and Sinatra Park, it provides recreational spaces for residents and hosts community events. Accessible and densely populated, Hoboken maintains its charm as a city with a unique blend of history, culture, and picturesque landscapes.
